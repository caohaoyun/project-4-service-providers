About Us

We are a team of four skilled data scientists, graduates of the University of Toronto's School of Continuing Studies, on the brink of completing our final project. Our project focuses on predicting property prices in select East Coast states of the United States, utilizing advanced machine learning techniques and data visualization tools. Our goal is to offer valuable insights to homeowners, investors, and policymakers, showcasing the power of predictive analytics and data visualization. As we share our findings, we invite you to join us on this journey into the world of data science and machine learning, where innovation and impact are our guiding principles.


You will be able to locate our Linkedin URLs by clicking in our name or picture. Also, feel free to check out GitHub profiles by clicking our username, under our names.
